About Us

We are career mentors & advisors to dentists or dental students who are seeking to practice dentistry in USA and want to make an informed decision about the realities of life after graduation.

How it works

At Dentalxplorer, we help aspiring dentists like you get the support you need to work in the United States.

Our mentoring program pairs you with an established dentist here in the United States and that mentor helps you understand the different parts of dental practices in this country.

Your mentor will take you in and teach you all the different procedural considerations here in America, they will work with you on understanding the cultural practices expected of American dentists and their patients, and finally, you will leave with an understanding of how to successfully practice dentistry in the United States.
